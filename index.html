About Us

Korata Transport Services is a professional transport service company based in Gauteng, South Africa. We specialize in providing safe and reliable transportation for learners, ensuring they get to and from school securely. We also provide safe and affordable transport to the employees of entities with late working hours. With our fleet of well-maintained Suzuki Ertiga vehicles, we also offer Uber services with improved safety. When riding with our drivers you know who is your driver.

Our Mission

At Korata Transport Services, our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation for learners, ensuring parents have peace of mind knowing their children are in good hands. We also improve the safety of using uber services and transport services for our beloved people. Safety is our priority.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted transportation service in South Africa, known for our commitment to safety and customer satisfaction.
